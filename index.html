Who We Are

Founded in 2008 by principal architect Rafiya Mensah-Acquah, our firm has grown from a two-person studio in Accra into one of West Africa's most celebrated architectural practices. We believe architecture is the most public of the arts — every building is a gift (or a burden) to the city around it.

We take that responsibility seriously. Our 60-person team of architects, structural engineers, project managers, and interior designers works as a single unit — never outsourcing, always accountable.

Our portfolio spans luxury residences, mixed-use commercial towers, cultural institutions, and public infrastructure across Ghana, Nigeria, Senegal, and beyond.
